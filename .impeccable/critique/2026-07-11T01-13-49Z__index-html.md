## Priority Issues

### [P1] Spanish recipe content remains incomplete

Some recipe descriptions or family notes fall back to English in Spanish mode. Make fallback status explicit and keep safety information authoritative.

### [P1] Inventory remains too administrative

Fifty-five rows still expose stock state and row menus. Optimize the default view for low/out exceptions and disclose full maintenance controls on demand.

### [P1] Week opens with editing before orientation

Place the seven-day summary strip before the selected-day form so users see the week before editing it.

### [P2] Grocery actions compete

Keep Add item primary, group scan/generation under Import, and subordinate clear/delete management.

### [P2] Add source cards imply sequence

Replace numbered photo/URL cards with a direct source chooser if another iteration is warranted.

## Persona Red Flags

**Busy parent:** Week and inventory still require too many repeated decisions for one-handed use.

**Spanish-first caretaker:** English fallback recipe text can be mistaken for a complete translation, especially around family or safety notes.

**Low-confidence helper:** Stock meanings, weekly inheritance, and immediate removal lack enough explanation or undo.
